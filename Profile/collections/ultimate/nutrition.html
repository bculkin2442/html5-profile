Introduction to Physical Fitness and Nutrition

For years, everyone has known that regular exercise along with good nutrition is key to your health. The trick is how to include these elements into your busy schedule. The stress of modern times mandates you develop and maintain a fit, trim, and fully functioning body. Being active and fit also increases self expression and self esteem.

Research polls indicate that people today are becoming more health centered. As a result, people are becoming more interested in making fitness exercise an integral part of their lifestyle. This article will introduce you to the why of fitness, You will learn all about exercise and its benefits and will also learn how to structure a personal fitness program that is safe, reasonable, effective, and, above all, rewarding and fun.

Give some thought to the following statements:

  • • In the past, health meant only absence of disease. Today, we have a much broader perspective and consider physical finess to be a key component of total health.
  • •The modern lifestyle fosters unfitness because technological advances have eliminated much fitness producing physical exertion for many activities.
  • •Everyday activities, even for the laborer, no longer adequately stimulate the vital organs to provide physical benefits.
  • •Society, especially the corporate world, is beginning to realize the importance of health promotion and the importance of exercise in developing and maintaing good health.
  • •Being fit means living at your fullest physical potential. Physical fitness is the capability of the organs to work at peak efficency, allowing you to live a full and rewarding life.
  • •The basic components of physical fitness are cardio-respiratory endurance, strength, muscular endurance, flexibility and body composition.
  • •To be physicallly fit doest take effort, but exercise doesn't have to be painful to give rewards.
  • •Regular and vigourous exercise of the total body is a necessary ingredient of muscular and circulatory fitness - the key to fitness
